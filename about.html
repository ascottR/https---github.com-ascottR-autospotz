What is AUTOSPOTZ ?

Autospotz is an innovative automated parking system that offers customers an efficient and elegant solution for their parking needs. With a wide network of parking facilities across the country, Autospotz has quickly become one of the fastest-growing shares in the Colombo stock market since its listing. The company has garnered recognition from the government of Sri Lanka, receiving prestigious honors and medals for its unique and valuable services that contribute to both the country and the environment. Currently, Autospotz is expanding its operations with investments from both the government and the private sector.

Who are the founders ?

In the bustling urban cities plagued by parking woes, five ingenious undergraduates harnessed their creative thinking to craft a remarkable solution. Inspired by their own struggles, they conceived an automated parking empire. Through unwavering determination and entrepreneurial spirit, they turned their idea into reality, revolutionizing parking on land.
